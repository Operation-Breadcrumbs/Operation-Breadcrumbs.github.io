Tagline

Sincerely, Robin is a management, cooking, and narrative game set in a world of birds! Play as Robin and return to your hometown to regrow the bed and breakfast left to you by your late grandfather. Cook meals for your guests, engage with the community, and forge lasting relationships with the birds that come to stay! While traversing the tree, you will be able to interact with a variety of characters, create dishes to serve your guests, expand your B&B, and support your community.

Meet the team:

Nice to meet you! Fifty Birds in a Trench Coat, or Fifty Birds for short, is a Massachusetts-based group of game developers who love birds and putting virtual birds into virtual situations. Our goal is to create rich and wholesome games that reflect how our generation sees the world around us. As recent college graduates, we are breaking into the game industry with our first project Sincerely, Robin. Coming soon!
