Sincerely, Robin is a management and cooking simulator following Robin, a bird returning to their small hometown after inheriting their grandfather's old bed and breakfast. The player will combine ingredients to add flavors to their dishes, customizing each dish for each guest. All the while, they must deal with ever-increasing rent that threatens not just their business, but the well-being of the town itself.

Meet the team:

Nice to meet you! Fifty Birds in a Trench Coat, or Fifty Birds for short, is a Massachusetts-based group of game developers who love birds and putting virtual birds into virtual situations. Our goal is to create rich and wholesome games that reflect how our generation sees the world around us. As recent college graduates, we are breaking into the game industry with our first project Sincerely, Robin. Coming soon!
