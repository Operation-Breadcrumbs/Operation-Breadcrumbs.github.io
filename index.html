Sincerely, Robin is a management and cooking simulator following Robin, a bird returning to their small hometown after inheriting their grandfather's old bed and breakfast.

Combine ingredients to add flavors to your dishes, customizing each dish for each guest!

All the while, you must deal with ever-increasing rent that threatens not just your business, but the well-being of the town itself.

Meet the team:

Nice to meet you! Fifty Birds in a Trench Coat is a Massachusetts-based group of game developers whose goal is to create rich and wholesome games that reflect how our generation sees the world around us.
